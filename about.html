About

My Story

I was born and raised in the Washington, D.C. Metropolitan Area. I attended Frostburg State University and focused on Communications and Graphic Design. After college, I developed a hat business designing the hat brims of my customers. My freelance work involves designing flyers, banners, backdrops and portrait illustrations for clients in the Metropolitan Area. It took me years of switching from one field to another to realize that I need to stick to my true passion, creating and designing. For fifteen years, I have been focused on small business projects while going to school and eventually pursuing a career in marketing and sales. Recently, I enrolled into CareerFoundry in pursuit of a career in UI Design. This illustrious program has helped establish my understanding of the reason for designs and focusing on a different style of marketing and web creation. I specialize in graphic design, developing user interfaces, and graphite and charcoal portrait drawings. The combination of design, marketing and communication has given me the opportunity to effectively collaborate with people in the D.C. Area. With my background, I make sure I consider all research necessary to make sure your product attracts your target audience. Using my knowledge of marketing strategies, interpersonal and business communication, and design aesthetics gives me the opportunity to engage with all fields and apply it to a product.
